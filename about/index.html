About Randomly Pick

Randomly Pick is a browser-based toolkit for fast and simple random decisions. It includes Wheel of Names, Lucky Draw, Ladder Draw, Coin Flip, and Dice Roll.

What We Focus On

  • Fast input workflows for names and options
  • Clear, reproducible draw results
  • Practical tools for giveaways, classrooms, team splits, and events
  • Simple export and history handling where available

How It Works

Most tool interactions are processed directly in your browser for speed and usability. Randomly Pick is designed as a lightweight web app without account sign-in requirements.
